ALERT: This is a Tax Refund Scam!

You almost clicked on a fake IRAS notification. This is one of the most common scams in Singapore!

üö© Red Flags in This Scam:

  • Unexpected Refund: Claims you're owed money you didn't know about
  • External Links: Real IRAS communications direct you to myTax Portal, not external links
  • Urgent Deadline: Creates pressure with "forfeiture" threats
  • Email Notification: IRAS typically notifies via mail or myTax Portal inbox
  • Asks for Bank Details: IRAS already has your bank info from previous filings
  • Generic Greeting: Real IRAS letters use your full name and tax reference
  • Suspicious Email Address: Check if it's really from @iras.gov.sg domain

‚úÖ How to Verify Tax Refunds Safely:

1. Log in directly to myTax Portal at mytax.iras.gov.sg
2. Check your Notice of Assessment (NOA) for official refund information
3. Call IRAS hotline 1800 356 8300 to verify any claims
4. IRAS will NEVER ask for bank details via email
5. Refunds are automatically processed to your registered bank account
6. Be extra cautious during tax season (March-April)
