STOP! This is a Traffic Fine Scam!

You almost clicked on a fake LTA traffic fine notification - a very common scam in Singapore!

üö© Red Flags in This Scam:

  • Email/SMS Notification: Real LTA fines are sent via registered mail to your home address
  • External Payment Links: Official fines are paid through OneMotoring portal only
  • Partial Vehicle Number: Scammers use partial numbers to make it seem legitimate
  • Threatens Arrest: LTA doesn't issue arrest warrants for unpaid fines via email
  • Urgent Payment Pressure: Creates panic with threats of immediate consequences
  • Generic Address: Real notices include your full name and registered address
  • Vague Location: Real notices include precise location with photo evidence

‚úÖ How to Verify Traffic Fines:

1. Real traffic fines are sent via physical mail to your registered address
2. Check fines at official OneMotoring.com.sg portal
3. Call LTA hotline 1800 225 5582 to verify any fine notice
4. Real notices include your full name, NRIC, and vehicle details
5. Official notices include photo evidence of the offence
6. Payment is ONLY through OneMotoring or AXS stations
7. Never click payment links in emails or SMS
8. LTA will never ask for payment via external websites
